    Nine Sections. One Complete Picture.

    Every ShrinkSmart report includes up to nine analysis sections tailored to the selected department — from executive KPIs to the specific item-level action each manager needs to take this week.

    📊

    Executive Summary

    Total loss, weekly averages, annualized projections, and top findings in a format shareable with leadership without explanation.

    Leadership Ready
    🗑️

    Items Destroyed

    Every written-off item ranked by loss dollar. Over-production, coding errors, and ordering inefficiencies surface immediately..

    High Impact
    📉

    Items Discounted

    Items reduced in price to avoid waste, ranked by total dollars lost. Reveals ordering and assortment misalignment patterns..

    Ordering Insight
    ⚠️

    Dual Problem Items

    Products appearing in BOTH destroyed and discounted lists. These represent systemic failure and deliver the fastest ROI when fixed.

    Highest Priority
    🚨

    Zero-Sales Items

    Items with zero net sales still being ordered and written off. Stop ordering these immediately — pure preventable waste.

    ⏱️

    Security & Data Protection

    Enterprise-Grade Security Built In From Day One

    Grocery chains operate with sensitive operational and financial data. ShrinkSmart was designed from the ground up with the security requirements of enterprise clients in mind.

    Request Access →
    🔒
    Role-Based Access Control
    Store Managers see only their store’s data. District Managers see only their district. No user can access data outside their permission level.
    🔐
    Session Isolation
    No data is ever shared between user sessions. One store’s upload cannot be seen by another store’s user — at any time, under any circumstances.
    🛡️
    Secure Password Hashing
    Passwords are never stored in readable format. Industry-standard hashing protects all user credentials.
    🔑
    Encrypted Data Transmission
    All files transferred over HTTPS. Data encrypted both in transit and at rest.
    🗑️
    Zero File Retention by Default
    Uploaded files are never written to disk. Raw data is processed in memory and discarded automatically — your file never touches our servers permanently.
    📋
    Full Audit Logging
    Every login, upload, and report download logged with timestamp and user ID — a complete audit trail for compliance.

    What ShrinkSmart does — and doesn’t do

    ShrinkSmart gives your team the clearest possible picture of where loss is happening, which items are driving it, and what actions to consider. It doesn’t reduce shrink. Your team does. The platform’s value is entirely dependent on the follow-through at every level — from the department manager adjusting a production par to the district manager holding locations accountable week over week.

    ⓘNo guarantees, no projections. ShrinkSmart does not guarantee any specific reduction in shrink or dollar savings. Every chain is different. Results depend on your team’s engagement with the data and their willingness to act on what it shows. We make the problem visible. What your team does with that visibility is what drives the outcome.

    We’re not here to drop a report on your desk and walk away. ShrinkSmart is built by someone who has worked in this industry and understands the real-world pressures your team faces. Whether you need help interpreting the data, building a rollout plan for your stores, or getting your team aligned around what the numbers are showing — we’re available. That’s part of what you’re signing up for.
